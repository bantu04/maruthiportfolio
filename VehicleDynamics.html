    Cornoring Analysis

  • Conducted derivative analysis to assess vehicle stability and handling during cornering, adjusting the centre of gravity and cornering stiffness to achieve underdamped, critically damped, and overdamped responses.

  • Utilized ADAMS software to create a bicycle model for simulating yaw rate responses, confirming the underdamped condition stabilized quickly, while the critically damped and overdamped conditions provided enhanced stability.
