Bit about me

A Mechanical Engineering graduate from MLR Institute of Technology. I led a team in designing and optimizing the powertrain system for an SAE Supra car. We custom-mounted a Honda CBR 150R engine, cooling system to maintain optimal engine temperatures, and an efficient fuel system.

My interest in programming languages began when I enrolled in an AI workshop in my 3rd year B.Tech. Through the workshop I learned Python, Machine Learning, Algorithms, & AI and subsequently chose an AI project for my final year. We developed an AI-driven X-ray baggage scanning system using YOLO and Faster R-CNN algorithms. I further honed my skills in Python and Java by developing a personal AI voice assistant. This hands-on experience helped me secure my first job as an Application Development Associate at Accenture.

Additionally, I organized sports event in my undergrad third year, showcasing my leadership and event management skills. I also worked at Hyundai, MG Motors, and Tata Motors service sites, installing paint booths for quick car painting and drying. This involved drilling, welding, wiring, and insulation tasks. I strive to learn new technologies. I believe engineering is not limited to its core disciplines, It's about solving the real world problems.
